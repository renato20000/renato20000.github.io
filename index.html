About Us.

Queens Nails and Spa, established in 2014, is a premier nail salon located in Highland Park at 1896 First St. The spacious and extra clean salon features comfortable pedicure massage chairs, ensuring guests have a relaxing and rejuvenating experience. With a commitment to using top-quality beauty brands, Queens Nails and Spa guarantees beautiful and lasting results. Under the guidance of professional and licensed owners who boast over 10 years of experience in the beauty industry, the salon has flourished. The new management, led by Tracy and Hai, has further enhanced the salon's reputation, attracting loyal clients who appreciate the exceptional service and meticulous work provided by the talented technicians. From perfect no-chip mani/pedis to innovative dipped manicures, Queens Nails and Spa offers a wide range of services to suit all preferences. Clients rave about the salon's friendly staff, outstanding work, and the relaxing atmosphere that makes every visit a pleasure.
